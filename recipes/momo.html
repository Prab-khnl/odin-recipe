Momo Recipe


Description


Momo is traditionally a Tibetian dish, which has been influenced by Nepali food culture. It is a very popular dish and loved by almost every Nepalese.In this dish, a ball of Vegetable/Non-Vegetables is wrapped by a round sheet of dough.Momo is served with pickle as per test of who consumes it.


Ingedient to prepare Momo


For Momo:-


  • Cabage
  • Onion
  • Corriander
  • Paneer
  • Chili
  • Spices
  • Wheat Flour
  • Water
  • Oil

For Pickle:-

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Chili
  • Spices
  • Corriander
  • Oil

Steps to prepare Momo


  1. We prepare dough first. For that, add water into the wheat flour and prepare dough.
  2. For stuffing,grade the onions,cabbage and paneer.
  3. Mix the mixuture with the spices and add salt as per your taste.
  4. Heat the oil, and pour the oil into mixings.
  5. Mix the filings again.
  6. Press the dough into thin round shape.
  7. Add the filings and wrap the dough into round or crooked shape.
  8. Warm up the water in momo sheet.
  9. Place the uncooked momo in momo sheet.
  10. Prepare the pickle by cooking all ingredients together. Grind the pickle.
  11. After 10 minutes, take out momo and serve the momo in plate with pickle.
  12. Enjoy your tasty Momo!
